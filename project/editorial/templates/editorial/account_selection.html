Account Options

There are two ways to use Facet. Select which account best suits your project and goals.

Organization
Features
  • Add and manage your organization's team
  • Create and manage projects, series, stories and versions of stories.
  • Create networks and invite other organizations to participate.
  • Communicate at group, individual and content levels.
  • Track and manage metadata around partnerships and content.
  • Track various projects and priorities through dashboards.

Contractor
Features
  • Be publicly listed and discoverable as a freelancer.
  • Create and manage pitches to editors and track their progress.
  • Communicate directly with editors, respond to calls for pitches.
  • Create and manage the editorial process for assignments.
  • Be added to organization's projects and content.
  • Manage work and progress through dashboards.
